Emma Earl

Weddings . Elopements . Lifestyle . Photography

I’m a full-time photographer and shameless dog mom based in sunny Arizona. This is my 7th year as a photographer and I couldn’t imagine doing anything else.

When I don’t have a camera in my hand, you can find me planning my dog’s next birthday party, pretending I don’t like The Bachelor (and then watching every episode) or trying to keep my plants alive. To my core, I believe that overalls will never go out of style, you can never have too many to-do lists (virgo alll day) and sushi dates are the best kind of dates. Aside from capturing my adventurous + wildly in love couples, photographing humanitarian efforts and educating photographers are some of my biggest passions.

As a lover of places unknown and feelings not yet felt, traveling the world to document love stories is what makes me feel most alive. I’ve been able to travel to near and far away places for my job such as Thailand, Italy, Cabo and Canada – I jump at any and all opportunities to explore the world. Put me on a plane with Chick-fil-A in hand and I am one happy camper.

I am for the client who isn’t afraid to get a little messy, rejects traditional and cares more about the authentic moments than the posed. Capturing those candid moments between you + your loved ones is what gets my heart beating fastest. I serve those who throw caution to the wind and care less about the “extras”. The focus should be on your love – what this once in a lifetime day is truly about. If this sounds like you, I’m your girl. Shoot me an email!
